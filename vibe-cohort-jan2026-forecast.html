Key Insights

✅ Margin stabilises at 86–88% from LM2 onward

After January's AI spike is absorbed, the cohort's economics are very stable. By December 2026, margin is expected at 88.8% because AI costs continue to decline while monthly subscribers pay a consistent renewal rate.

📊 Annual plan is the backbone — 78.6% of base by Dec 2026

Annual plan subscribers start at 51.2% of the premium base and grow to 78.6% by December because monthly plan churns at ~11-12%/month while annual churn is only ~2%/month mid-cycle. Annual plan holders are very sticky until the 12-month renewal decision.

💰 Cumulative revenue by Dec 2026: ~$514K from 86,452 sites ($5.95/site)

$269K was collected in January alone (annual plan upfront payments). The remaining $245K comes from monthly renewals over 11 months. After the LM1 AI spike, this cohort is profitable every single month.

🔍 Watch: monthly plan churn rate in Jul–Aug 2026

The forecast assumes monthly churn stabilises at ~11%. If it continues declining toward 8–9% (as seen in some older cohorts), Dec 2026 active monthly sites would be ~360 (vs. 311 in base case), adding ~$1,200/month in revenue. Conversely, if it spikes back toward 18–22%, the monthly base shrinks faster.
